Instructions

Step 1: Create an Account

Click on the "Sign Up" button and fill out the registration form.

Step 2: Log In

Enter your email and password to log in to your account.

Step 3: Upload Your Data

Go to the home page and use the "Upload/Submit Your File" card to upload your data.

Step 4: Start Annotation

Click on the "Start Annotation" button in the Annotation card to begin the annotation process.
